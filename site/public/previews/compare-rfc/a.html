Inline header selectors

Each user-controlled column gets its own dropdown inside the table header. The 3rd column starts as a picker; click + compare more to reveal columns 4, 5, and 6 (each a fresh dropdown). The two fixed columns (opchain, Claude defaults) never move and never get a remove button.

UX note: dropdowns live in the table header, so when you swap a platform the column re-renders in place. Removing a column shifts the others left. The dashed + compare more column is visually quiet until you hover.
