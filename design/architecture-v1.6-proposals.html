Incorporating the 1.6 release into the architecture diagrams

v1.6 “the instrumented pipeline” adds two cross-cutting skills — oc-cost-ops (per-phase $ attribution · budget gates · model-tier routing) and oc-telemetry-ops (opt-in local usage metering) — taking the catalog 22 → 24. Because they measure every phase rather than sit on the spine, each proposal below places them differently. All three keep the existing diagram rules and add one new semantic accent — --inst lime — the v1.6 analog of v1.5’s violet AI accent.
