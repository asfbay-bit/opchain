What you’re evaluating. C makes the pipeline’s cost + usage legible per phase, live — the strongest “instrumented” feel of the three placements. Trade-off: it adds the most geometry (a rail column on desktop, a chip on every band on mobile) and introduces animation that the /architecture Lighthouse perf budget will police. If you pick C, the punch list swaps the Desktop-A band / Mobile-B pills for this rail + chips, plus a reduced-motion fallback (meters render final totals, no streaming) and a static-data path (the real meters read the checkpoint cost / telemetry_handle wire-1.1 fields, not a simulated loop).
