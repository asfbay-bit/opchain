Incorporating the 1.7 “Seams & Signals” release into the architecture diagrams

v1.7 adds three skills, taking the catalog 24 → 27: oc-signal-forge (question → trustworthy metric — the analytics backend), oc-modularize-ops (decompose a monolith with provable zero loss), and oc-fleet-ops (deploy + operate containers on self-managed infra). They form two new throughlines — a Signals chain (app-architect → signal-forge → dash-forge / monitoring-ops) and a Seams chain (modularize-ops → migration-ops → fleet-ops, with deploy-ops as a peer). One new semantic accent — --sig magenta — marks every 1.7 addition, the analog of v1.5’s violet AI accent and v1.6’s lime instrument accent. Below: 4 desktop (interactive) and 4 mobile (static) ways to view the updated diagram.
