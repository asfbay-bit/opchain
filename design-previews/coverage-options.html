What opchain knows.

Every stack-forge pack opchain ships, grouped by what role it plays in the dispatch graph. Languages have a canonical test-runner / build / lint command. Frameworks attach to a language pack. Mobile packs dispatch through a release checklist. Deploy-targets are the leaves of the dispatch graph.
